What is KAJSEC?

KAJSEC is a cybersecurity oriented community located at Kajaani, Finland. Our purpose is to organize meetups and get-togethers focused on training and networking. Anyone interested in the Infosec field is welcome to join us, meet likeminded people and hopefully learn something new.

Join KajSec – Strengthen Your Cybersecurity Network!

Are you passionate about cybersecurity?
Looking for a local community to exchange knowledge, discuss the latest threats, and improve your skills?
Kajaani CitySec is the place for you!

We are a friendly and open group of cybersecurity enthusiasts, professionals, and beginners alike. Whether you're a seasoned expert or just starting your journey in infosec, you'll find value in connecting with like-minded individuals.

Why Join?

Knowledge Sharing – Stay ahead of the latest cyber threats and best practices.
Networking – Meet local professionals, ethical hackers, and security researchers.
Hands-on Learning – CTF challenges, workshops, and practical sessions.
Community – A supportive space to discuss ideas and career growth.
